Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

01 Introduction

Promptly ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using the Promptly Chrome extension. As the "AI Memory Layer," we specialize in helping you govern AI workflows while prioritizing a secure, local-first experience.

02 Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you sign up for cloud sync, we collect your email address and name via Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Workflow Data: We store your saved prompts, categories, and workflow templates. This data is stored locally on your device and, if signed in, synced to your private cloud storage via Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Subscription Data: We use Lemon Squeezy to manage professional subscriptions. While we do not store your credit card details, we maintain your subscription status and billing email to verify Pro tier features.
  • Usage Stats: We track local metadata like prompt usage counts and "Last Used" timestamps to help you organize your own data. This metadata is never shared with third parties.

03 How Your Data is Used

Your data is used exclusively to facilitate your AI workflows:

  • To provide the core functionality of prompt saving and injection.
  • To synchronize your library across different browsers and devices.
  • To manage and verify your professional subscription status.

We do not track, store, or monitor your actual conversations with AI models (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini). Promptly only interacts with the prompts you explicitly save to its library.

04 Permissions Explanation

Promptly uses the following permissions for specific functions:

  • Storage: To cache your prompts locally for instant offline access.
  • Host Permissions: To enable the Sidebar injection on AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.

05 Third-Party Services

We leverage industry-standard infrastructure to ensure security and reliability:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication and real-time cloud synchronization.
  • Lemon Squeezy: Used for secure payment processing and subscription management.
